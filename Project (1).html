MY STORY

MY NAME IS OSCAR QUINTANILLA, I'M 24 YEARS OLD AND ORIGINALLY FROM EL SALVADOR. WHEN I WAS 8 YEARS OLD I STATED SKATING FOR THE VERY FIRST TIME. AS I KEEP GROWING UP MY LOVE AND DESIRE OF BECOMING A PROFESSIONAL SKATER WERE BIGGER AND BIGGER. I REMEMBER MY DAYS IN EL SALVADOR WHEN I USE TO SKATE EVERY SINGLE DAY WITH MY FRIENDS, I REMEMBER LEARNING FROM THEM AND HOW MUCH SUPPORT THEY GAVE ME. WHEN I WAS 15 YEARS OLD I MOVED TO THE UNITED STATED. YEARS LATER I STATED MY CAREER AS A TECHNOLOGY AND INFORMATION MAJOR, BUT PART OF MY HEART WILL FOREVER MISS MY DAYS OF SKATING. ONE DAY I DECIDED TO RECORD MY FRIENDS AND MYSELFT SO WE CAN ALL HAVE MEMORIES OF THOSE DAYS WHEN WE USED TO DO WHAT WE ALWAYS LOVED IN CASE ONE DAY IN OUR LIFE WE GET TO THE POINT WHERE WE ARE NOT ABLE TO PRACTICE SKATEBOARDING ANYMORE. I HOPE YOU LIKE MY VIDEO AND ENJOY THEM AS MUCH AS ME AND MY FRIENDS DO.
